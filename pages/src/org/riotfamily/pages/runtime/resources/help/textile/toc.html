Textile

A Humane Web Text Generator

what does it do?

Quick block modifiers:

Header: hn.
Blockquote: bq.
Numeric list: #
Bulleted list: *

Quick phrase modifiers:

_emphasis_
*strong*
??citation??
-deleted text-
+inserted text+
^superscript^
~subscript~
%span%

To apply attributes:

(class)
(#id)
{style}
[language]

To align blocks:

< right
> left
= center
<> justify

To insert a table:

|a|table|row|
|a|table|row|

To insert a link:

"linktext":url

To insert an image:

!imageurl!

To define an acronym:

ABC(Always Be Closing)
